About this site...

There are few things that I love in this world more than a good movie quote! When I was a child I wanted to be a writer. I would spend hours a day coming up with the "perfect" way to denote an image, a feeling, a state of being, etc. However, aspirations change. While, I no longer want to be a writer, I like to spend my time attending plays, listening to music, and watching movies! In doing these things, I like to observe people's choice of words when they speak. This page is dedicated to providing you with a list of some of my all time favorite movie quotes!
